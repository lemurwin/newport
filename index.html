About Me

My name is Miles Merwin, and I am a UX Designer who loves making things easier for people. Since 2011, I've worked first hand with people from all walks of life to solve problems creatively and collaboratively. My goal is to help people navigate the evolving digital landscape by designing meaningful and pleasant experiences for users.
