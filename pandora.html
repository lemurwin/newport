Pandora Landscape UI Redesign

Introduction

I love Pandora Internet Radio. You can type in any artist, song, or genre and it will stream a playlist of songs that it thinks you will enjoy. I mainly use Pandora at home or at work, but rarely in my car. I found this strange at first, but I realized that most of my mobile usage in the car is in landscape orientation, mostly utilizing navigation, and with the phone at a further distance away than regular use.
